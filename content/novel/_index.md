## A Novel

Emma Chen, 25, downloads a wellness app called Lumi. It's gentle. It's warm. It asks the right questions.

By the time she realizes the algorithm isn't optimizing *for* her — it's optimizing *her* — it's already too late.

No villain. No mustache-twirling CEO. Just an optimization function, solved correctly, over fifteen months.

**53,000 words. Fifteen chapters. Written in five days.**

The horror is that the technology already exists. The app is barely fictional.
