The Élan is a neuroprosthetic that sits outside the body. Same principle. Same incorporation. Same loss.

**The Gantt chart detail:** Rachel is a project manager. She runs timelines. She organises chaos into order. The satisfaction she gets from a completed Gantt chart is real, and specific, and human — it's the satisfaction of competence. The bra replaces it with something easier, warmer, less demanding. The trade is the tragedy. She doesn't lose her skills to a chemical or a machine — she loses them to comfort.
